About Me

I am a pragmatic software engineer who enjoys building clean applications and solving complex problems. My main tech stack consists of React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, SQL, Java, C++, HTML, and CSS.

I am fascinated by how an idea can come to fruition with the technologies offered today. Being a software engineer gives me the freedom to develop things that were inconceivable in the past and challenges myself to stay up to date on the ever-changing world of technology. It truly amazes me how millions of lines of code change the way we interact with the world and I hope to be a part of this change.

On a more personal note, I am a semi-professional pc gamer, whiskey enthusiast, and love building computers for family and friends. In my free time I like to read up on the latest tech regarding phones and computers.
